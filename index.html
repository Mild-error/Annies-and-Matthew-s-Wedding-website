How We Met

Annie and Matthew first met while working in the same company in 2018. Matthew almost instantly left the department he was originally employed for and joined the Testing team where Annie was. On Matthew's 1st day in testing team, he approached Annie very late in the afternoon and said: "Hey, let me help you ;)" (with twinkles in his eyes!). Annie couldn't help but take on his offer. This was the beginning of their journey together. Annie and Matthew have exchanged countless memes, shared the same interest with music, gaming and most importantly, FOOD! And since, the rest is history.

We can't wait to celebrate with you at our wedding ceremony and reception in June 2025!
